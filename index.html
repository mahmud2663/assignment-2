Concept in architecture is an idea, thought, or notion that is the main element that
drives a design project forward. It is the backbone and foundation of an architectural
project. The progress of a design depends on its concept. It is the first step a designer
takes before the start of his/her project. That is why concepts are said to be the seed
of a project that later on develops into a full-grown tree. A concept doesn’t have to come out
from a single source of knowledge..
